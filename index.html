What I do

User experience & problem solver

I love watching ideas come to life. I use my favorite tools to create user journeys, wireframing, flows and prototyping. I am a fan of getting stuff done and involving users in the process.

I have worked with almost every step of the website process, since I started designing over twenty years ago. Today, I am Head of Design at the Aarhus based web company No Zebra and spend my days moving the UX & Design department forward.

Back Design Systems

Bringing order to chaos with design systems

I have worked with design systems and Atomic Design for several years, and I'm very passionate about the methodology. Lately I have been using with Fractal, in fact, this site was made using that: ds.brianstefan.dk

I wrote a blog post (in Danish) a while back, about how we did it, while I was at Novicell.

Apart from being a collection of guides, standards and reusable components, a design system is also a great tool for collaboration and communication. Furthermore it can help creating better user experiences, testing and more. Ask me about design systems!
