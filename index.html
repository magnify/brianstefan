UX Engineer & problem solver

User Experience is in my opinion a discipline that, depending on the task, can require a multitude of skills. The mindset, method and being able to put yourself in your users shoes, is in my opinion the key to success when working with UX.

Through the years, that I have been working with the internet, I have aquired lots of different skills. I have designed, wireframed and developed a lot of websites, I have prototyped apps and I was even a SEO expert once.

But most of all, I am a problem solver.

Today I use my accumulated skills for solving challenges of warying kinds. I put myself in the users shoes, and use whatever tool that is in my toolbox when solving and delivering a good experience.
